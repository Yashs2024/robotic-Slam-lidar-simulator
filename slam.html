All Features

Everything You Need to Learn Robotics

A professional-grade simulator packed with real-world SLAM algorithms, interactive controls, and stunning visualizations.

Core SLAM

📡

LiDAR Sensor Simulation

180° forward-facing LiDAR with adjustable ray density, sensor noise, and real-time distance measurements in metres.

🧮

Bayesian Log-Odds Grid

Professional-grade occupancy mapping using Bayesian probability. Watch cells gain confidence with gradient shading as the robot scans.

📈

Odometry Drift

Simulate real-world wheel encoder errors. The believed position diverges from ground truth — the fundamental challenge of localization.

🔗

Loop Closure Detection

Scan-matching keyframe system detects revisited areas and corrects accumulated drift — the defining feature of real SLAM.

Navigation & Control

⚡

A* Pathfinding

Heuristic shortest path through the occupancy grid with wall proximity penalties for safe navigation.

🔍

Dijkstra's Algorithm

Guaranteed optimal path without heuristics. Compare with A* to understand the speed vs. optimality tradeoff.

🐛

Bug2 Wall-Following

A reactive algorithm that follows walls to reach goals. Works without a map — perfect for unknown environments.

🧭

Frontier Exploration

Autonomous exploration that finds boundaries between known and unknown space, mapping everything systematically.

🚗

3 Drive Models

Differential (tank), Ackermann (car-like), and Holonomic (omnidirectional) — each with unique kinematics.

Advanced Systems

🎯

Particle Filter (MCL)

Monte Carlo Localization with adjustable particle count. Watch hypotheses converge as the robot gathers evidence.

🔮

Dynamic Obstacles

Moving walls, sliding doors, and orbiting objects test the SLAM system's ability to handle changing environments.

🌫️

Fog of War

Unexplored areas remain hidden on the SLAM map, revealing only what the robot has scanned — just like reality.

Tools & Visualization

🌐

3D LiDAR Point Cloud

Interactive Three.js visualization with distance-colored points, orbit camera, and live scan rays in 3D space.

📊

Live Sensor Charts

Real-time polar chart of LiDAR distances plus a Sensor HUD showing pose, min/max distances, and compass.

🏗️

Map Builder & Presets

Draw custom walls or load preset environments — Maze, Warehouse, Office, L-Shape, Open Field. Export/import as JSON.

📖

Interactive Tutorial

Guided 11-step walkthrough teaching every SLAM concept with auto-scrolling, view switching, and clear explanations.

🔊

Sound Effects

Subtle audio feedback for LiDAR sweeps, collisions, goal reached, and exploration complete.
